# Introduction to Driving

## Welcome to Driving School

Welcome to our driving school! This course will provide you with the essential knowledge and skills to become a safe and responsible driver.

### Basic Concepts

- **Steering**: Learn how to control the steering wheel.
- **Braking**: Understand the importance of proper braking techniques.
- **Acceleration**: Master the art of smooth acceleration.

### Road Safety

- **Signs and Signals**: Recognize and understand road signs and signals.
- **Lane Discipline**: Maintain proper lane discipline to ensure safety.
- **Speed Limits**: Always adhere to posted speed limits.

### Practical Driving

- **Parallel Parking**: Learn how to park your vehicle in a parallel space.
- **Three-Point Turn**: Master the three-point turn maneuver.
- **Emergency Stops**: Practice emergency stops to ensure safety in critical situations.

### Code of Conduct

- **Respect Other Drivers**: Always respect other drivers on the road.
- **Use Turn Signals**: Use turn signals to indicate your intentions.
- **Avoid Distractions**: Do not use your phone or any other distractions while driving.
